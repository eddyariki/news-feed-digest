## Fact-Check Report — 2026-02-25

### Part 1: News Digest
**Verdict:** PASS WITH NOTES

#### Issues Found

**[Google Disrupts UNC2814 GRIDTIDE Campaign After 53 Breaches Across 42 Countries]** — The digest states the group "breached at least 53 organizations across governments and telecoms on **five continents**." The source JSON summary specifies only three geographic regions: "Africa, Asia, and the Americas" — which constitutes at most four continents (counting North and South America separately), not five. Europe and Oceania are not mentioned in the source. Correction: change "on five continents" to "across Africa, Asia, and the Americas."

### Part 2: Paper Summaries
**Verdict:** PASS WITH NOTES

#### Issues Found

Papers.md reads "…Adriana Szekeres, **Suman Nath (Microsoft Research), Kexin Rong (Georgia Tech)**" but both the PDF and the arXiv JSON source record the order as "…Adriana Szekeres, **Kexin Rong, Suman Nath**." The PDF lists: **Che Wang, Fuyao Zhang, Jiaming Zhang, Ziqi Zhang, Yinghui Wang, Longtao Huang, Jianbo Gao, Zhong Chen, Wei Yang Bryan Lim.** Correction: restore the paper's byline order.
